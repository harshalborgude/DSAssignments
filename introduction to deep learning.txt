
## Types of deep learning 
1) ANN - Artificial neural network
    a. classification 
    b. Regression
2) CNN - Convolution neural network
    - Input data will be in the form of images & video frames 
3) RNN - recurrent neural network 
    - used for NLP , if input in form of text , timeseries data
    - word embedding , LSTM RNN , GRU RNN , Bidirectional LSTM RNN , encoder & decoder , transformers & Bert 
    - Framework : tensorflow (Goodle) , Pytorch (FB)
